# smokebud — twitter buzz kit

Launch thread material. Each tweet stands alone; pick one as the opener or run
them as a drip over launch week. Media files referenced live in this folder.

---

## Tweet 1 — the hook

> it's 2am. you don't want to talk. you don't want a feed.
>
> you want someone to stand outside with.
>
> smokebud is a website where a companion smokes with you for five quiet
> minutes. that's it. that's the whole app. 🚬🌙

**media:** `ritual.webm` (the break scene in motion — ember flares, smoke curls)

## Posting notes

- Voice: lowercase, unhurried, no exclamation marks — the product whispers.
- Best posting window: late evening local time; the premise lands at night.
- Convert `ritual.webm` to mp4 for Twitter: `ffmpeg -i ritual.webm -c:v libx264 -pix_fmt yuv420p ritual.mp4`
- Alt text for images: "a dark website showing a silhouetted companion smoking
  on a balcony over a sleeping city, thin smoke rising."
